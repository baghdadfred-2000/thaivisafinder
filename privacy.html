Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

We take your privacy as seriously as you do. This is the honest, plain-English version of how Thailand Visa Finder, operated by Genext Information Systems, handles information.

What we collect

Almost nothing about you personally. The Visa Finder runs entirely in your browser. The answers you give the questionnaire are used on your device to generate your result — they are not sent to our servers, sold, or used to build a profile of you.

Local storage

We may store a small amount of data on your own device (via cookies or local storage) to remember your cookie choice and, where offered, your last result so you don’t have to start over. This stays on your device. You can clear it any time through your browser settings.

Advertising & cookies

This site may display advertising served by Adsterra. To do this, Adsterra and its advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies:

  • Third-party vendors, including Adsterra, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • These advertising cookies enable Adsterra and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You can review Adsterra’s practices in its Privacy Policy. You can also opt out of some third-party vendors’ use of cookies for personalised advertising at aboutads.info/choices.

Where required by law, advertising and analytics cookies are only set after you give consent through our cookie banner, and you can withdraw consent at any time.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting, aggregated analytics to understand which pages are useful and improve the tool. Any analytics are configured to avoid collecting personally identifying information wherever possible.

Third parties

We load web fonts from Google Fonts, may serve ads via Adsterra, and link out to official Thai government sites and our sister site. These third parties have their own privacy policies governing the data they process. We encourage you to review Adsterra’s Privacy Policy.

Your rights & contact

Because we hold little to no personal data ourselves, there is usually nothing to request — but you can clear local storage and cookies any time via your browser. If you have a privacy question, contact the operator, Genext Information Systems, via our Contact page.

Changes

We may update this policy as the site or the law evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the “last updated” date above.
